Frequently Asked Questions

What types of cases do you handle?
We provide expert legal services across a wide range of areas, including:
• Criminal Litigation: Defending clients in cases like theft, assault, or fraud.
• Civil Litigation: Resolving disputes such as property or contract issues.
• General Litigation and ADR: Offering mediation, arbitration, or court representation.
• Estates Planning and Wills: Drafting wills and managing trusts.
• Family Law and Matrimonial Matters: Handling divorce, custody, and maintenance.
• Medical Negligence: Pursuing claims for substandard medical care.
• MVA and Personal Injury Claims: Securing compensation for accidents or injuries.
• Contracts and Lease Agreements: Drafting and reviewing agreements.

How long does it take to resolve a legal dispute?
The timeline depends on the case type and complexity. Mediation or arbitration can resolve disputes in weeks or months, while litigation may take longer, especially if it proceeds to court. We strive to resolve matters efficiently, keeping you informed at every stage. During your consultation, we’ll provide a realistic timeline based on your case.
How do I challenge and unfair dismissal?
If you believe you were unfairly dismissed, you can refer your case to the Commission for Conciliation, Mediation and Arbitration (CCMA) within 30 days of dismissal. We assist with preparing your case, gathering evidence, and representing you in CCMA proceedings to seek reinstatement or compensation. Our litigation expertise ensures your rights are protected.
How do I know if i need a lawyer?
If you’re facing a legal issue—whether it’s a criminal charge, a family dispute, an injury claim, or a contract concern—a lawyer can protect your rights and guide you toward the best outcome. We offer an initial consultation to assess your situation and advise on the next steps. Contact us to discuss your case.
What is mediation, and is it right for my case?
Mediation is a voluntary, confidential process where a neutral mediator helps parties resolve disputes without going to court. It’s cost-effective, faster, and less adversarial than litigation, making it ideal for family, civil, or commercial disputes. Our team is skilled in mediation and can advise if it suits your needs.
What should I expect in a divorce or a family law matter?
Family law matters like divorce, child custody, or maintenance require sensitivity and expertise. We guide you through each step, from negotiation to court representation, ensuring fair outcomes. Expect an initial consultation to discuss your goals, followed by tailored advice on property division, parenting plans, or spousal support.
Can you help with drafting a will or planning my estate?
Yes, we specialize in estates planning and wills. We draft clear, legally sound wills and establish trusts to protect your assets and ensure your wishes are honored. Our personalized approach considers your family and financial circumstances, offering peace of mind for you and your loved ones.
How do I claim compensation for medical negligence or car accident?
For medical negligence, we investigate whether a healthcare provider’s substandard care caused you harm, pursuing compensation for damages. For MVA claims, we handle Road Accident Fund (RAF) applications or third-party claims to secure fair compensation. We manage the entire process, from evidence collection to settlement or trial, ensuring your recovery is prioritized.
What are the costs of your legal services?
We strive to provide affordable, transparent legal services. Costs vary based on the complexity of your case and the services required (e.g., consultation, litigation, or drafting). We offer an initial consultation to discuss fees and payment options. For certain cases, like personal injury claims, we may work on a contingency basis (no win, no fee). Contact us for details.
